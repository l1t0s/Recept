Ingredient Amount
Eggs 3 pcs.
Bacon 150g
Oil 50ml

  1. Step 1

    Put a pan on a stove

  2. Step 2

    Pour an oil on a hot pan

  3. Step 3

    Put bacon slices on a hot pan

  4. Step 4

    Fry bacon till it turns gold-brown color and has a crisp (like on a picture)

  5. Step 5

    Take bacon out of a pan and put it on a towel

  6. Step 6

    Put eggs on a pan

  7. Step 7

    Fry them till they have crispy borders(like on a picture)

  8. Step 8

    Put all your ingredients on a plate

Bon Appetit!
